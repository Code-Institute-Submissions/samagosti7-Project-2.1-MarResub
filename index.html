Welcome to the online home of the classic game, rock, paper, scissors. Simply click play to get started! On the right, there is a key indicating which icons correspond to which. When you select one of the three, the computer will make its choice also, and the competition begins! Your score versus the computer will be logged in a results line below the game itself. Good luck!
